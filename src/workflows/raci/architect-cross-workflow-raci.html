üìä Key Patterns Across Workflows:

  • Systems Architect Consistency: Always Responsible (R) for architectural decisions across all workflows
  • Accountability Shifts:
    • Feature: Engineering Manager accountable for design decisions
    • Bug Fix: Engineering Manager accountable for fix approach
    • Hotfix: Emergency Approver accountable for all decisions
  • AI Persona Collaboration: Multiple AI architects work together, especially in Feature workflow
  • Governance Board Involvement:
    • Feature: Active consultation and approval
    • Bug Fix: Informed of systemic issues
    • Hotfix: Bypassed during emergency, informed post-incident

‚è±Ô∏è Time Investment by Role:

  • Feature Development: Systems Architect heavily involved (8-16 hours)
  • Bug Fix: Systems Architect moderately involved (2-4 hours)
  • Hotfix: Systems Architect rapidly engaged (30-60 minutes)
  • Other AI Personas: Consulted as needed, parallel processing capability

ü§ñ AI Persona Architecture Insights:

  • Orchestration Role: Systems Architect AI coordinates other AI personas based on need
  • Parallel Processing: Multiple AI personas can work simultaneously on different aspects
  • Knowledge Synthesis: Systems Architect combines inputs from specialist AIs into unified design
  • Continuous Learning: Pattern library grows from all workflows, improving future decisions
  • Emergency Response: AI can instantly engage for hotfixes, no "wake up" time needed
  • Consistency Enforcement: AI ensures standards compliance across all workflow types

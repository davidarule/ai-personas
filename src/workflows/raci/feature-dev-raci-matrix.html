Key Observations from the RACI Matrix:

  • Developer: Primary responsibility for implementation and addressing feedback, but rarely accountable for decisions
  • Tech Lead: Holds accountability (A) for most critical decisions and quality gates
  • Code Reviewer: Critical role in the review phase but minimal involvement elsewhere
  • Workflow System: Executes most automated processes and orchestrates the flow
  • Azure DevOps: Central hub for work tracking and PR management
  • CI/CD Pipeline: Responsible for all automated quality checks and deployments
  • Product Owner: Consulted during requirements and informed of progress
  • Monitoring Team: Only engaged post-deployment for operational health

Patterns Identified:

  • Clear separation between execution (R) and accountability (A)
  • Multiple responsible parties for critical operations (merge, rollback)
  • Product Owner has minimal direct involvement but stays informed
  • Monitoring Team engagement is concentrated in post-merge phase
